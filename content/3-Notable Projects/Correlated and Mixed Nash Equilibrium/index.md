### Project Overview
Implemented algorithms to compute **Correlated Nash Equilibria (CNE)** and **Mixed Nash Equilibria (MNE)** for strategic games. The project applies optimization and game-theoretic techniques to analyze rational decision-making and equilibrium strategies in multi-agent environments.

### Key Features
- **Mixed Nash Equilibrium:** Computed mixed strategy equilibria for two-player strategic games.
- **Correlated Equilibrium:** Implemented algorithms to determine correlated equilibria using optimization techniques.
- **Game Analysis:** Evaluated player utilities, strategy distributions, and equilibrium stability.
- **Strategy Visualization:** Visualized payoff matrices and equilibrium outcomes for comparative analysis.
- **Performance Evaluation:** Tested algorithms across multiple game scenarios and benchmark examples.

### Technologies Used
- **Python:** Core implementation language.
- **Game Theory:** Mixed and Correlated Nash Equilibria.
- **Linear Programming:** Optimization for correlated equilibrium computation.
- **NumPy:** Numerical computation and matrix operations.
- **Matplotlib:** Visualization of strategies and payoff analysis.

### Software Architecture
- **Game Engine:** Represented normal-form games and payoff matrices.
- **Equilibrium Solver:** Implemented algorithms for Mixed and Correlated Nash Equilibrium computation.
- **Optimization Module:** Solved linear programming formulations for correlated equilibria.
- **Analysis Module:** Compared equilibrium solutions and evaluated player payoffs.
- **Visualization Module:** Generated graphical representations of strategies and results.

### Impact and Applications
- **Game Theory Research:** Demonstrates practical implementation of equilibrium computation algorithms.
- **Multi-Agent Systems:** Applicable to AI agents and strategic decision-making problems.
- **Economic Modeling:** Useful for analyzing competitive and cooperative interactions.
- **Algorithm Design:** Showcases optimization techniques for solving complex strategic games.
